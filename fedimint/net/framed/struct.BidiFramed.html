Expand description

Framed transport codec for streams

Wraps a stream S and allows sending packetized data of type T over it. Data items are encoded using bincode and the bytes are sent over the stream prepended with a length field. BidiFramed implements Sink<T> and Stream<Item=Result<T, _>>.
