Backup and recovery of ecash notes

Ecash backup is implemented by periodically taking a snapshot, self-encrypting it and uploading it to federation.

Recovery is done by deriving deterministic ecash nonces and checking, them with federation. A successfully recovered snapshot can be used to avoid having to scan the whole history.
