SubscribeInterceptHtlcs opens a stream for a client to receive specific HTLCs that have a specific short channel id. For every HTLC intercepted and processed, the client should use CompleteHtlcs RPC to stream back a Success or Failure response.

Recommendation: GatewayLightning implementations should respond with a channel stream over which intercepted HTLCs are continually sent to the client.
